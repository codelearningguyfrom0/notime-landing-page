Our Story

嘆佬NO TIME is a Hong Kong local indie band established since 2016. 3 members with 3 different voices and instruments, they played various style of pop music from different eras in over hundreds of events and venues.

Dedicated to promote canto-pop, 嘆佬NO TIME released several original songs with touching lyrics and enchanting melodies.
